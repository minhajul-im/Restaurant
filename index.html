naif

A naif is a person with very little experience. When you're just starting a new job, you often feel like a naif. You might have noticed how similar the noun naif is to the adjective naive. They share a common origin, the French word naïf, which means both "natural, unspoiled, or innocent" and also "foolish." When you describe someone using the adjective form of naif — which can be used interchangeably with naive — you are usually implying that the person is a little childlike or immature.

About Us

Presenting a world of contrasts to city-goers, Hithe + Seek is an interactive and explorative wine bar and restaurant set in the heart of the City of London. Thoughtfully created as an experience for novices, wine connoisseurs and enthusiasts to discuss and share knowledge with our Wine Curator. The wine list is made up of 80 references, carefully selected from old favourites to new grapes and wine regions as an invitation to explore new territories. Made for sharing and pairing, the food menu has been designed by Executive Chef Jorge Colazo to perfectly enhance the wine experience. The full-length windows overlooking the Shakespeare's Globe and Tate Modern provide an abundance of natural light whilst plush furnishings and modern finishes create an intimate atmosphere. When the sun goes down, sip on carefully selected wine while the city lights up to offer a unique river setting.

Grapes
